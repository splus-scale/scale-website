# Data access info

All public datasets produced by this research group are indexed on this website. Each published article is associated with a dedicated page under the [Publications](/publications/) section. For organizational purposes, the public data corresponding to each publication are made available directly on the respective publication page.

Requests for access to unpublished data should be directed to the corresponding author of the article, using the email address provided.
