About the project

S-PLUS Clusters/Groups and their Large-scale Environments (SCALE) explores how galaxies grow, evolve, and interact within clusters, groups, and the vast cosmic web that connects them. SCALE combines 12-filter optical data with public spectroscopy and high-resolution imaging to reveal the structure and history of galaxies, from dense cluster cores to their outermost regions, extending up to five times the cluster's virial radius.
